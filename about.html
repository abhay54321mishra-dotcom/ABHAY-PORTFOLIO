About Me

A student of life, art, and everything in between.

AAFT · 2025
Who I Am

Abhay Mishra

I'm a multi-disciplinary creative currently studying at AAFT School of Animation, Marva Studio, Sector 16 — where I am learning to turn imagination into compelling visual experiences.

My journey as an artist is rooted in curiosity. I've always been drawn to multiple forms of expression — from the meditative patience of traditional oil painting to the electric energy of VFX compositing. I believe the best creative work happens when you refuse to stay inside one box.

At AAFT, I've been trained in the fundamentals of animation, visual effects, and digital media. Outside the classroom, I explore illustration, literature, and experimental art as ways to sharpen my voice and deepen my perspective.

I am available for freelance illustration, motion graphics, photo manipulation, and creative collaboration. If you're looking for someone who brings genuine passion and a multi-disciplinary eye to the table — let's talk.
